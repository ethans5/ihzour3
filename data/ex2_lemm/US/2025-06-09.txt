                  tribute to dr everett l. dargan m.d. 

                                  
                                 

                          hon james e. clyburn 

                            of south carolina 

                     in the house of representative 

                           monday june 

   mr. clyburn mr. speaker i rise today to pay tribute to dr. everett 
 l. dargan an outstanding surgeon a devoted husband and father and an 
 all around good citizen of south carolina dr. dargan pass away on 
 april and leave an endure legacy of stewardship 
 excellence and distinction he lead an inspiring life of remarkable 
 achievement and grace as a scholar surgeon sportsman public servant 
 mentor and friend to many 
   grow up in columbia south carolina during the great depression 
 dr. dargan support his family by shin shoe deliver 
 newspaper waiting table and working summer on his uncles&#x27 family 
 farm 
   at the age of dr. dargan win a scholarship to morehouse college 
 in atlanta he later transfer to the university of buffalo in 
 upstate new york where he earn a bachelor&#x27;s degree in biology in 
 dr. dargan continue his education at howard university&#x27;s college 
 of medicine where he earn his m.d. and win prize in medicine 
 after medical school he complete his internship at kings county 
 hospital center in brooklyn new york and be later name chief 
 resident surgeon at the bronx municipal hospital center of the albert 
 einstein college of medicine in new york 
   dr. dargan interrupt his specialty training to serve in the united 
 states air force as a captain and commander of the usaf hospital 
 in mildenhall lakenheath england during the korean war later he 
 would continue his commitment to provide quality medical care to 
 military veteran through his service to the dorn veterans 
 administration medical center in columbia south carolina 
   dr. dargan return to new york and continue his commitment to 
 academic medicine as a researcher and instructor through various 
 appointment include associate professor of surgery at the albert 
 einstein college of medicine chief of surgery at lincoln hospital and 
 director of surgery at sydenham hospital at lincoln hospital he meet 
 his wife a register nurse and flight attendant at twa carol poyner 
 they marry in and later move to boston where dr. dargan 
 complete his training in thoracic and cardiovascular surgery at boston 
 city hospital achieve the post of chief resident surgeon and become 
 a thoracic surgical instructor at boston university medical center in 
 massachusetts 
   in keep with his commitment to academic surgery as teacher 
 practitioner and researcher he teach medical student and surgical 
 resident for more than year dr. dargan be an advocate for 
 indigent patient care and quality medical care for veteran 
   in dr. dargan return to columbia for the remainder of his 
 career join the private medical practice of cyril spann 
 m.d. and then join in partnership with gerald a wilson m.d. to 
 form midlands surgical associates pa a private surgical practice he 
 remain dedicated to serve his fellow veteran with excellent 
 medical care practice at dorn veterans medical center for decade 
   dr. dargan be the african american surgeon to serve as chief 
 of surgery and then chief of staff at richland memorial hospital now 
 prisma health a lifelong scholar he be honor by the alpha omega 
 alpha honor medical society in for his distinguished career of 
 leadership in medicine after retirement from his surgical practice in 
 dr. dargan work for the south carolina board of medical 
 examiners for nearly a decade in dr. dargan receive the dean&#x27;s 
 distinguished service award as a friend of the university of south 
 carolina school of medicine 
   in a final act of service dr. dargan donate his body to the 
 department of anatomy at howard university college of medicine to aid 
 in the training of future medical professional 
   throughout his career he train fellow surgeon present 
 research finding and deliver paper at medical conference in 
 africa and throughout the united states often at meeting of the 
 american college of surgeons where he become a fellow and for the 
 national medical association he be a lifelong devoted member of zion 
 baptist church where he serve as a trustee 
   dr. dargan be renowned for his knowledgeable professional and kind 
 relationship with his patient he be a practitioner who offer his 
 patient holistic and nutritional advice a lot of which he learn 
 from his parent both of whom grow up on rural farm and particularly 
 from his dietician mother 
   the dargans have daughter jennifer dargan and catherine dargan 
 phelps a son in law peter phelps and grandchild addison 
 dargan phelps morgan dargan phelps and landon dargan phelps 
   mr. speaker i ask you and my colleague to join i in celebrate 
 the life and legacy of an incredible man and servant let we recognize 
 dr. dargan&#x27;s commitment to service sacrifice for and contribution to 
 this great nation
