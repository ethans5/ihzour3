                      honor dominican author week 

                                  
                                 

                        hon pablo jose hernandez 

                              of puerto rico 

                     in the house of representative 

                          saturday may 

   mr. hernandez mr. speaker i rise today with great pride to 
 recognize and celebrate the extraordinary contribution of dominican 
 writer during the official observance of the casa dominicana week of 
 the dominican author to be hold from may to may this 
 historic celebration be make possible through the joint effort of the 
 dominican ministry of culture dominican consulate in puerto rico 
 unesco dominican republic and pen international dominican republic it 
 be also thank to the collaboration of the senate of puerto rico house 
 of representatives of puerto rico and the municipal legislature of san 
 juan 
   the write word be among the most powerful expression of a person&#x27;s 
 soul through literature author preserve memory provoke thought and 
 illuminate the human condition dominican literature in particular 
 stand as a vibrant testament to the cultural richness resilience and 
 creativity of a people whose story transcend border and generation 
 puerto rico and the dominican republic share a deep connection and 
 culture our common spanish language reflect a historical struggle 
 literature serve as a testament to our history and the relationship we 
 have share between both country for century 
   this week as the representative of puerto rico in congress i join 
 the dominican republic to honor a distinguished group of dominican 
 writer both from the homeland and the diaspora who have make 
 exceptional contribution to the art and the global literary 
 community in addition the dominican community make essential 
 contribution to puerto rican society as business owner athlete and 
 great citizen today i be pleased to recognize the contribution of 
 the follow dominican honoree to the caribbean and global 
 literature 
   carlos roberto gomez beras miguel angel fornerin eugenio garcia 
 cuevas doris melo antonio nelson soto miguel patino ramon dario 
 jimenez 
   sebastian robiou lamarche ana a. marchena segura giovanni di 
 pietro israel mercedes romelinda grullon nelson del castillo dulce 
 elvira de los santos 
   grace pena rosario guzman pamela bernal dorothy bell ferrer 
 rafael mendez tejada 
   mr. speaker it be with great pride that i commend these 
 distinguished author on their well deserve recognition and i wish 
 they continue success in their future endeavor

                     remembering chairman bo mazzetti 

                                  
                                 

                            hon darrell issa 

                              of california 

                     in the house of representative 

                          saturday may 

   mr. issa mr. speaker i rise today to honor the extraordinary life 
 of bo mazzetti a veteran a veteran statesman and a relentless 
 champion for tribal sovereignty and the people of the rincon band of 
 luiseno indians 
   for over decade bo not only lead his own tribe but also help 
 lead california&#x27;s native nation into a more prosperous future know 
 for his steady hand quiet dignity and unmatched resolve bo be most 
 importantly a leader who never fail to listen from san diego county 
 to sacramento and all the way to the hall of congress he make 
 historic change to advance tribal sovereignty be it tribal water 
 right infrastructure modernization or unjust gaming compact bo 
 bring common sense solution to difficult problem 
   a vietnam veteran bo remain devoted to leadership and service from 
 the time he enlist in the navy and to those of we who have the honor 
 to serve and work alongside he he be a friend 
   bo&#x27;s legacy will live on in the land he protect the institution 
 he build and the generation he empower my prayer be with his 
 wife mary his child and the entire rincon community
