   mr. van drew mr. speaker as we celebrate catholic schools week 
 across the nation we come together to recognize and honor the profound 
 impact of catholic education on january we mark national 
 appreciation day for catholic schools a day that acknowledge the 
 endure commitment of these institution to educate not only the mind 
 but also the soul catholic schools like st. ann&#x27;s in wilmington 
 delaware where my grandchild attend instill faith academic 
 excellence and service in their student together we be not just 
 celebrate a week we be celebrate the noble mission of catholic 
 education to educate the whole child and transcend the boundary of 
 traditional learning god bless our catholic schools and god bless the 
 united states of america
