                        hon eleanor holmes norton 

                       of the district of columbia 

                     in the house of representative 

                         monday october 

   ms. norton mr. speaker pro tempore today i introduce the district 
 of columbia juror pay parity act which would require d.c.&#x27;s local 
 trial court the superior court to pay juror the same amount federal 
 court pay juror currently the superior court pay juror the same 
 amount as federal juror but the superior court have sometimes pay 
 less 
   under the national capital revitalization and self government 
 improvement act of congress assume responsibility for funding 
 the local d.c. court the local d.c. court however have always be 
 under the direct control of congress even when they be fund by 
 d.c. the d.c. home rule act prohibit the d.c. council from enact 
 any law with respect to any provision of title of the d.c. code 
 under title the board of judges of the superior court set juror 
 pay 
   i urge my colleague to support this bill
