  introduction of the commission of fine arts district of columbia home 
                                 rule act 

                                  
                                 

                        hon eleanor holmes norton 

                       of the district of columbia 

                     in the house of representative 

                           monday july 

   ms. norton mr. speaker today i introduce the commission of fine 
 arts district of columbia home rule act this bill would remove the 
 authority of the commission of fine arts cfa over non federal real 
 property in the district of columbia 
   under presidential executive order eo and federal law the cfa 
 have review authority for d.c.-owned park and building as well as for 
 certain private land in d.c. under eo the cfa have review authority 
 for the design of d.c.-owne park and building under the shipstead- 
 luce act the cfa have review authority for the design of private 
 building in d.c. ` ` adjacent to public building and ground of major 
 importance,&#x27;&#x27 such as rock creek park and the potomac riverfront under 
 the old georgetown act the cfa have review authority for the design of 
 private building in the part of georgetown refer to as ` ` old 
 georgetown&#x27;&#x27 in the act 
   the federal government include the cfa whose member be 
 appoint by the president should not have any authority over d.c. 
 land use policy and decision unrelated to the federal presence not 
 only be the federal government&#x27;s authority over d.c. building and 
 private property a violation of home rule it also delay and increase 
 the cost of public and private development in d.c. 
   this bill be an important step to increase home rule for d.c. i urge 
 my colleague to support it
